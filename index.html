Tibits is a vegetarian restaurant I discovered when I moved to Switzerland.

Unlike a traditional restaurant where you come and order food, it works with a buffet concept. You come, choose your food and can either have it inside or order to take away.

Unlike some buffets I have been to before, in Tibits food always tastes fresh and delicious. Tibits claims "We value cooking with fresh, natural products. tibits treats are glutamate-free, without artificial additives and free of genetically modified foods. All baked goods, dairy products and free-range eggs are of organic or Demeter quality." I can attest.

On top of that, food is amazingly healthy, and time-demanding to cook. Last but no least, the decoration full of plants and cozy is very inviting. I always love my visits to Tibits.
